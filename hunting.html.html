WHAT IS HUNTING?

  • HUNTING lets you send your character out to track down monsters and creatures, giving you a chance to earn coins and other currencies, find loot drops like materials or chests, gain Hunt EXP to level up your Hunt Level, and occasionally encounter stronger enemies with better rewards.
  • Your Hunt Level and hunt-related upgrades can increase the coins you get, improve your chances of finding rare loot, and slightly boost the odds of triggering special hunting events.
  • There is a cooldown between hunts, so you’ll need to use it strategically and combine it with your other progression commands.

HUNTING INFORMATION.

  • In order to start a HUNTING game. First, you must use command /hunt, then the bot will send a message that contains 4 buttons: HUNT Hunt Stat Equipment Team
  • After that just press the HUNT and then you will see an Idle Message [cooldown] of 3s before the HUNTING game starts.
  • Finally after 3s cooldown ends, the Hunting Message will appear. This is where you will start the PVP between Creatures and Player

The message will contains 3 stuff: Battle Canva, Action Panel and Action Message.

  • Battle canva: This is an image that is generated by Bot, it will show the creatures, user and your army/pet. Each will be inside a card, each each will have a health bar, level info, image and status symbol [when active].
  • Action panel: This is a selection panel that allows player to choose which creature to attack. You only have 2 actions per turn so choose wisely.
  • Action message: This is a message place where it will show what had happened, show either player attack action or creature attack actions. It even show the status effect when u have.

NOTES:

  • If your HUNTING game was left idle for 30 seconds, the bot will automatically make the hunt fails.
  • If you don't have any tool/gear used in your EQUIPMENT, the bot will automatically equip Fist for you. Means you will use your barehands to attack them, but it is not recommended!
  • Also don't try to use many commands and do as many HUNTING game as you can because it won't work ;]

HUNT's ATTACK TYPES

There're currently 3 types of attacks: Singular, AOE [Area of Effect] and Critical Attack.

  • Singular: This attack only allow either player or creature to target and deal damage to 1 enemy.
  • AOE: This attack will affect all player and allies or creatures. The one that being attacked with AOE will receive 100% damages while the others receive 45% damages only.
  • Critical Attack: This attack can be used in either Singular or AOE, it only has 15% chance to occurs a Critical Attack. A critical attack will deal 25% more damages than the original [means +25% damages]. But for AOE, the one being targetted will only gain 25% bonus damages, while others still receive only 45% damages.

PLAYER/CREATURE HUNTING INFORMATION

PLAYER STAT INFORMATION:

Player stat can be accessed by pressing Hunt Stat button. Upon pressing, the bot will edit the message into this:

The message includes 3 info: Player Level + Progress Bar, Health, Defense.

  • Player progress bar will have their own set equation for XP requirement per level:

    100 + 50 * {level}

  • Player level is capped at level 100. Means when reaching level 100, player cannot be able to proceed.
  • For every time player leveled up, player will gain 25 more Health.
  • FACT: total Hunt XP needed to reach level 100 is 257,500 XP and total health is 2600 Health

CREATURE STAT INFORMATION:

Each creatures will have their own health, status, and attacks.

  • For every level the creature gain, their health, damage, coins reward, and hunt reward will increase by 50%, 25%, 10%, and 15% respectively.
  • Each creature has a level range from 1 - 12, means level 12 is max level.

But player can only counter a specific creature level and rarity base on their Hunt Level:

Hunt Level Creature Level Highest Rarity
0 - 10 1 RARE
11 - 20 1 - 3 EPIC
21 - 35 1 - 5 LEGENDARY
36 - 50 1 - 7 MYTHICAL
51 - 80 1 - 9 SECRET
80 - 100 1 - 12

EFFECTS

There's currently only 1 effect in hunting feature:

POISON
  • A status effect, it will decrease your health overtime. But never reach 0.
  • Poison will decrease user health by {set}% per round, base on each creature setting.

HUNTING GEAR & MISC

In hunting game, not all gear or misc items are elligible to use for hunting. Player can only use gear or misc items that are tagged with HUNTING

In order to change your gear or equipment, just press Equipment button and this message will appear. It will contain 2 selection panel for you to choose, one is to select a Gear/Weapon and one is to select a Misc item.

Please note that all gear/weapon has Durability Stat so besure to check the gear durability before using, otherwise you may break a gear/weapon if the durability is very low.
